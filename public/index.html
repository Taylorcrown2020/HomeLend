How it works

From numbers to your front door

STEP 01

Build your profile

Tell us your target home, income, debts, and assets. We exclude the debts that don't count — like installment loans with 10 or fewer payments left, and medical debt.

STEP 02

See it underwrite live

A sticky bar tracks your front and back DTI, LTV, estimated payment, and cash-to-close as you type, with Texas property tax filled in automatically from the ZIP.

STEP 03

Shop what you qualify for

Create your secure account, submit, and land on a property dashboard filtered to your qualified price range. Save the homes you love.
